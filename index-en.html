Provide support for the development and operation of Huaxin Group's products and services in the gaming, cultural and creative, e-commerce, education, technology and other industries. The company is currently affiliated with the "Yingshanhong" studio, which mainly focuses on two game-related businesses:

    1. Development, operation and marketing of overseas research and marketing platform www.gamcoo.fun;

    2. Research, development and operation of "Zhongyong Game" (tentative name). The main idea of "Zhongyong Game" is to use gamification scenarios. Through joint activities, such as simulating part-time work, traveling together, completing tasks, etc., it sets specific decision-making, interaction, and gaming situations, allowing players to fully experience the playability of the game. At the same time, we slowly realize the true meaning of "golden mean" and achieve a harmonious life of happiness and love.

    Founded in 2011, the company is a leading Internet user experience research company in China; a member of the China Association of Market Information Research (CAMIR).

    Provide customers with full-case services such as product research, market research, research execution, and marketing planning consulting. Particularly good at qualitative and quantitative user experience research and consulting in the technology product industry and Internet products.

    The company adheres to the truth-seeking and pragmatic research philosophy and is committed to providing customers with effective market research and execution services. At present, the company's business scope covers the Internet, home appliances, FMCG, government public services, online games, real estate, daily chemicals and other industries.

    Official Website

    The company mainly engages in the development and operation of the "Xinyouba" and "Huaxinshe" platforms, as well as the development and operation of mini games.

    "Xinyouba" has been the first game joint research platform in China since 2020. It currently has more than one million hard-core game players of all types, and it collects about 10,000 valid questionnaires on a daily basis. It provides 100+ game co-research tasks every month, and distributes around 1 million yuan in various co-research mission gifts, recommendation fees, etc. to players every month.

    "Huaxinshe" is a user experience research platform specifically targeted at non-game products such as e-commerce and technology. At present, the platform is mainly responsible for co-research tasks on Douyin, mobile phones, and technology products. It has accumulated more than 500,000 users, distributes 20+ co-research tasks every month, and distributes various co-research task gifts and recommendation fees to users of about 300,000 yuan. .
